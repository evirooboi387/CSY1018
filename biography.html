Biography

Riwaj Bastola is an enthusiastic and a passionate learner. This 19 years old grew up in the beautiful city Dharan situated at the eastern part of the country. He completed his SLC from Vijayapur Higher Secondary School and his +2(11 and 12) from CCRC College. He is also highly devoted towards sports and football, basketball and tennis are his favourites. Currently he is studying BSc. (Hons) Computing in NAMI and further plans to do his masters on Software Engineering aiming to become a successful web developer in near future.
From his early childhood he was very eager towards computers further encouraging him to take computer science in his secondary level. Witnessing his urge towards computers, his parents took him to Kathmandu, the city where opportunities lies between, for his higher secondary study.
